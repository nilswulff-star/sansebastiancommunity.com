Development Progress

Project Timeline For
San Sebastian Development

Phase One · Active

Phase One — 56 Lots

Phase 1 of the construction project is currently underway and comprises a total of 56 lots that are ready for commencement. Anticipated completion: April 2025.

Phase Two · In Progress

Phase Two — 72 Lots

This phase encompasses an additional 72 lots, with the primary roadway extended to connect with North Oscar Williams Road. Construction commenced Q1 2025, projected completion Q4 2025.

Phase Three · Coming Soon

Phase Three — 51 Lots

The concluding phase encompasses 51 additional plots with an extension of the primary road providing additional access to West Business 77 Road.
